Ardipithecus

  • Lived between 5.8 million and 4.4 million years ago
  • Bipedal
  • Originated in Ethiopia
  • A relatively flexible lumbar region with a lumbar curve, short and broad pelvis, with a wide sacrium
  • Fact: Never make it out of Africa
    • Source: https://australian.museum/learn/science/human-evolution/ardipithecus-ramidus/

Australopithecus

  • Lived between 2-4 million years ago
  • Originated in Eastern Africa
  • Height: about 1-1.7m
  • Sexual dimorphism was more exaggerated than in modern humans
  • carnivorous diet
  • Brain size: 1/3 of humans
  • Source: https://open.lib.umn.edu/humanbiology/chapter/1-7-the-evolution-of-primates/

Paranthropus

  • Lived between 2.4-1.4 million years ago
  • Originated in Eastern Africa and Southern Africa
  • Height: about 1.3-1.4m
  • Characteristic: large grinding teeth, vegetarian diet, extensive tool use
  • Highly sexual dimorphic, bipedal
  • Brain size: less than 1/2 of humans
  • Source: https://australian.museum/learn/science/human-evolution/paranthropus-species/
  • Source: https://www.sci.news/othersciences/anthropology/dnh-155-paranthropus-robustus-09035.html

Homo Habils

  • Lived between 2.4-1.4 million years ago
  • Originated in Eastern Africa and Southern Africa
  • Height: about 1-1.35m
  • Characteristic: jaw that was less prognathic than the australopiths, long arm, bipedal, extensive tool use
  • Brain size: 1/2 of humans (600–750 cubic centimeters)
  • Source: https://vocal.media/humans/the-evolution-homo-habilis
  • Source: https://australian.museum/learn/science/human-evolution/homo-habilis/

Homo Erectus

  • Lived between 1.8 million years ago
  • Originated in Eastern Africa, Northern Africa, Southern Africa, Central and Eastern Asia, and perhaps Europe
  • Fun fact: first hominin species to migrate out of Africa, likely ancestors of humans
  • Height: about 1.4-1.8m
  • Less sexual dimorphism
  • Appearance: human-like body proportions and upright stance, a protruding brow ridge, large face and no chin
  • Meat and plant diet
  • Source: https://capudinos.medium.com/homo-erectus-top-1-62d3590657df
  • Source: https://australian.museum/learn/science/human-evolution/homo-erectus/

Homo Sapiens

  • Lived between 500,000 years ago
  • Originated in Africa
  • Height: about 1.8m
  • Appearance: short base and a high braincase, face is reasonably small with a projecting nose bone, eye sockets are square, jaws are short
  • Brain size similar to modern humans (1,200–1,400 cubic centimeters)
  • Source: https://australian.museum/learn/science/human-evolution/homo-sapiens-modern-humans/

Homo Neanderthalensis

  • Lived between 400,000 to 40,000 years ago
  • Originated in Europe, southwest and central Asia
  • Height: about 1.5-1.75m
  • Diet: meat, plants and fungi
  • Brain size similar to modern humans (1,200–1,750 cubic centimeters)
  • Appearance: large nose, short body
  • Source: https://australian.museum/learn/science/human-evolution/homo-neanderthalensis/

Source: https://education.nationalgeographic.org/resource/global-human-journey/
